BOURBON KITCHEN

<div class="Prep">
<p>
<h3>
<ul style="list-style-type:none;">
<li>Prep time: 5 mins</li>
<li>Cook Time 15 mins</li>
<li>Total Time: 20 mins</li>
</ul>
</br>
Serves: 4
</br>
</br>
<i>Super Easy One Pan Bourbon Chicken. Serve with rice and noodles to make it a meal!</i>
</h3>
</p>
</div>
</br>
<h2>
<b>INGREDIENTS</b>
</h2>
<div class="ingrediants">
<p>
<h4>
<ul style="list-style-type:square;">
<li>½ teaspoon sesame oil</li>
<li>1 tablespoon canola oil</li>
<li>2 pounds chicken breast, cut into 1 inch cubes 4 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li>1 teaspoon ginger, minced</li>
<li>¼ cup low sodium soy sauce</li>
<li>¼ cup light or dark brown sugar</li>
<li>2 tablespoons bourbon</li>
<li>¼ cup rice wine vinegar</li>
<li>1 teaspoon cornstarch</li>
<li>1 tablespoon water</li>
<li>sesame seeds for garnish, if desired</li>
<li>green onions for garnish, if desired</li>
</ul>
</h4>
</p>
</div>
</br>
</br>
<h2>
<b>INSTRUCTIONS</b>
</h2>
<div class="instructions">
<p>
<h4>
<ol>
<li>Heat sesame oil and canola oil in a large skillet.</li>
<li>Add in chicken and saute until fully cooked and browned.</li>
<li>Add in garlic and ginger and saute for 1-2 minutes. (Add in more oil if needed)</li>
<li>In a medium bowl combine soy sauce, brown sugar, bourbon and rice wine vinegar.</li>
<li>Add sauce to the chicken and bring to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes.</li>
<li>Meanwhile in a small bowl whisk together cornstarch and water.</li>
<li>Slowly add the cornstarch mixture to the sauce and stir frequently until thickened, 2-3 minutes.</li>
<li>If the sauce needs to thicken more add more cornstarch</li>
<li>Serve immediately with sesame seeds and green onions for garnish, if desired.</li>
</ol>
</h4>
</p>
</div>
